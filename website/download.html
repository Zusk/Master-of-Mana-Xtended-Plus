Download for Master of Mana 1.3 (about 470mb)

Download Instruction

First, you need Civilization IV and the Expansion Beyond the Sword (BTS), both installed. Make sure you also have Patch 3.19 for CIV IV BTS installed.

Then Download Master of Mana 1.3 and execute the file. A Setup Programm will guide you through the installation. The install path must be the "\mods\Master of Mana" subfolder of your CIV IV BTS folder, but the install programm should point you to the correct folder.

As a final step you should take a look at the announcement section of the forum for any new patches released!
