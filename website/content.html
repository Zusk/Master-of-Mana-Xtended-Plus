Patch 1.40 "Promotions" released

Promotions and Equipment have been modified a lot.

Equipment doesn't need to be repaired anymore and only higher level Units can use it. Lower level Units get a passive Combat Bonus
that depends on how many Resources are hoarded. This cuts down a lot of boring Micromanagement.

A lot more Promotions can now be gained in battle, for example Units may gain Poison Resistance when they defeat
Goblins using Poison. Disciplines and Classes allow a high degree of Unit Specialization.
