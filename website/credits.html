Work from other Civilization IV Modifications (mods)

There is a lot of Art/code/lore from Fall From Heaven II used. So huge thanks to the Fall from Heaven team and everyone who contributed to it or one of its many modmods.

Several Improvements to the User Interface were merged from the popular BUG (Better Unaltered Gameplay) Mod for Civilization IV.

Modular XML Loading was taken from the World of Civilization (WOC) mod.

Performance tweaks were merged from the CAR mod.

And finally a good amount of Content was inspired or directly merged from several Fall From Heavon modmods, in particular Fall Further (FF)

Work from Artists

A lot of work is taken either from the Internet or other Civilization IV mods that do not have a complete Credit list, so unfortunatly I cannot provide one either.
